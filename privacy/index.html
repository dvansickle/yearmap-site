Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2026

YearMap is a Chrome extension developed by Spring Harbor Studios. We believe in simplicity, privacy, and transparency.

Extension Data

YearMap stores your display settings, categories, range notes, and calendar ranges via Chrome’s storage APIs. This data is saved in your browser and may optionally sync across your Chrome profile if you have Chrome sync enabled. We do not receive, sell, or share this extension data.

Extension Analytics and Tracking

The YearMap extension does not embed analytics, trackers, cookies, or third-party monitoring scripts. The extension does not collect web browsing activity, page content, or personal identifiers.

Website Data

This website uses Google Analytics to understand aggregate site traffic and page usage. If you email support or use an external donation link, the information you provide is handled by that service according to its own privacy policy.

Chrome Web Store Limited Use

The use of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Chrome Web Store User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. We use extension permissions only to provide YearMap’s user-facing calendar, settings, storage, and export features.

External Links to Support or Donations

YearMap may provide links to external websites for support or donations, such as our Buy Me a Coffee page (buymeacoffee.com/dvansickle) or support email links. Clicking these links opens the external site; any data you share on those sites is handled under their privacy policies.

Children’s Privacy

YearMap is intended for general users and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as YearMap evolves. If we make significant changes, we’ll update the date above and post the new version at this same URL. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

Contact

If you have privacy questions or concerns, email support@yearmap.app.
