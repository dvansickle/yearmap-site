FAQ

Does it replace my new tab?

It can. YearMap is designed to greet you when you open a tab, but you can turn new-tab replacement off and open the calendar in a regular tab from the extension icon.

Can I align weekdays vertically?

Yes. Toggle the weekday-aligned layout in Settings. Changes apply instantly.

How do notes work?

Right-click a range lane to edit its dates and note together. Saved notes appear in the sidebar preview, hover tooltips, and ICS event descriptions.

What about deletions?

Hold Shift and click any day in a range to remove it. Use Ctrl/Cmd+Z within 30 seconds to undo an assignment, edit, or deletion.
