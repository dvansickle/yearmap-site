Privacy

YearMap runs entirely within your Chrome profile. Data is stored using Chrome storage (with optional sync across your devices). We do not collect analytics or sell/share personal data. ICS export files are generated locally in your browser.

FAQ

Does it replace my new tab?

You can choose: use YearMap in every new tab or open it on demand via the extension icon.

Can I align weekdays vertically?

Yes. Toggle the weekday‑aligned layout in Settings. Changes apply instantly.

How do notes work?

Right‑click a range to edit a note. Notes auto‑save and appear in hover tooltips and ICS event descriptions.

What about deletions?

Hold Shift and click any day in a range to remove it. Use Ctrl/Cmd+Z within 30s to undo.
